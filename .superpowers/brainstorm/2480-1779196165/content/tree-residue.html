How it works (briefly)

  • Canopy positions are computed from the tree algorithm. Each leaf in the canopy becomes a spawn point for residue.
  • Residue is the existing leaf primitive, but spawn points are tied to the tree's canopy (not random). Particle count derives from 2depth × spread_factor(angle).
  • On tree-mode close, the new params are written to localStorage and the residue recomputes its spawn positions + density.
  • On first load (no localStorage), default residue ≈ depth-8, angle-27°, shrink-74% (the current tree defaults).
  • Reduced motion / mobile: leaves are static-rendered (no drift), residue still scales with params.
