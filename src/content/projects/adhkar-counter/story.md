This little app is much like a widget. It was one of my simplest apps to put together and just meant to address a personal problem of mine. I wanted to put together this little Adhkar Counter app to have a little web-app on my laptop to persistently track as I did Adkhar. After some messing around, it was pretty easy to put together in Swift (and seems to be pretty lightweight!). To be honest though, I think I might need to rethink what this would be used for, given that trying to do Adhkar or remembering to do it while working on something separate may not be an effective or consistent use case. I’ve thought about developing it to use during breaks or to add text and readable Adhkar, basically rethinking the exact way the app is meant to be used, while keeping the primary use case for those who may want to use it as such.
