For a while, I’d considered whether the addictiveness of Instagram was worth the ability to stay connected to friends by messages and keep up. Eventually, I’d decided to delete my account and had kept it like that for a while. InstaDM was developed to make it so I, firstly, could only access the messages via this app while staying off the rest of the app, like reels and the feed. While I may not always use it, I do think it’s a cool app to have and that it could be useful to people. This is why I worked to get it on GitHub so that the app is usable to people who want to stay connected via messages on Instagram while avoiding some of its addicting elements.
