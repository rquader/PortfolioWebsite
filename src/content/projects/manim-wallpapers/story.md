At this point, I’d gotten inspired by the cleanly animated and deeply impressive videos by 3Blue1Brown which he put together using his own Python library, Manim. Around the same time, I’d discovered an app that allowed me to have animated wallpapers on my Mac. These two things coming together put an idea in my head. If I gave Claude Code access to Manim and asked it to develop well-made wallpapers, what would it do? That was the start of the “Manim Wallpapers” project. To be honest, as of writing this, I don’t actively use any of the Manim wallpapers on my laptop, but they firstly look very, very cool and they served as a basis for the recursive tree on the first page of this portfolio site and are pretty satisfying to look at if you go through the image carousel with the animations!
